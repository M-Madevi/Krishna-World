Family of Lord Krishna

A concise, devotional family overview—parents, foster-parents, siblings, spouses, and children.

Tip: Filter works across all sections below.

Parents & Guardians

Biological Parents

Vasudeva (father)
Devaki (mother)

Krishna was born to Devaki and Vasudeva in Mathura.

Foster Parents

Nanda (father)
Yashoda (mother)

He was lovingly raised in Gokul & Vrindavan by Nanda and Yashoda.

Siblings

Balarama

Elder brother (Baladeva). Revered for strength and devotion.

Subhadra

Sister of Krishna & Balarama; wife of Arjuna; mother of Abhimanyu.

Extended Yadava Kin

Includes many cousins and clan members in the Yadu dynasty.

Principal Queens (Aṣṭa-Bhāryā)

Traditional lists speak of eight principal queens at Dwarka.

Rukmini

Foremost queen; regarded as an incarnation of Lakshmi.

Satyabhama

Renowned for courage and devotion.

Jambavati

Daughter of Jambavan; mother of Samba (well-known).

Kalindi

Personification of the Yamuna; mother of Shrutakarma.

Mitravinda

Princess of Avanti; marriage narrated in Purāṇas.

Nagnajiti (Satya)

Daughter of King Nagnajit of Ayodhya.

Bhadra

A principal queen; traditions vary on her lineage notes.

Lakshmana

Won by Krishna in a swayamvara contest.
