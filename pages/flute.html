🎵 Krishna’s Flute – The Voice of Divine Love

Not just an instrument — Krishna’s flute is the voice of love, surrender, and soul.

  • 🪈 Symbol of Divine Call: The flute's melody draws Gopis, animals, sages — all creation listens and surrenders.
  • 🌸 Power of Love: With just a note, Krishna steals hearts, melts pride, and awakens devotion.
  • 🧘‍♂️ Ego-less Soul: The flute is hollow — reminding us to be empty of ego, so that the Divine can flow through us.
  • 💗 Heart Connection: Radha hears it and forgets the world. It’s the call of true, eternal love.
  • 📿 Bhakti Symbol: Saints like Mirabai, Surdas, and Jayadeva praised Krishna's flute in poetry and song.

“Let your heart be like Krishna’s flute — empty of ego, filled with love, and always singing the Divine's tune.”
