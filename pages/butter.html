🧉 Krishna’s Butter Pot

✨Symbol of Innocent Joy: The butter pot reminds us of Krishna’s childhood —
full of mischief, sweetness, and pure love.

👶Lila (Divine Play): As a child, Krishna would steal butter, symbolizing His delight in
the innocent offerings of love from His devotees.

📜Scriptural Meaning: “He who stole butter from every heart” — Krishna doesn’t
desire wealth, but the ‘butter’ of devotion, churned from pure hearts.

🧈Spiritual Essence: Butter is soft, smooth, and sweet — just like divine love.
Let your heart melt like butter in Krishna’s hands.

🪔Life Lesson: Be light like butter, soft in heart, sweet in words, and rich in love.
Krishna accepts what is offered with feeling — not form.
