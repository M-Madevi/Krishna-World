🔆 Sudarshan Chakra – Krishna’s Divine Weapon

⚔️ Power of Protection: The Sudarshan Chakra is the divine discus weapon of Lord Vishnu and Krishna, known to destroy evil and uphold righteousness.

🔱 Eternal Symbol of Dharma: It represents the never-ending cycle of time and karma. It spins endlessly, cutting through ignorance and injustice.

📖 In Scriptures: Krishna used the Sudarshan Chakra in the Mahabharata to protect His devotees. It obeys only His will and moves faster than light.

🌟 Meaning: The chakra teaches us to protect truth, act swiftly against wrong, and always revolve around Krishna, just as it does.
