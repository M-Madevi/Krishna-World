🪷 Krishna’s Sacred Lotus

🌼 Symbol of Purity: The lotus blooms untouched by muddy waters — just as Krishna radiates pure love amidst worldly chaos.

🧘 Spiritual Awakening: Like the lotus that rises toward the sun, Krishna lifts the soul toward truth, peace, and devotion.

📖 From Scriptures: Krishna’s eyes, hands, and feet are described as lotus-like — soft, radiant, and full of grace.

💖 Inner Message: The lotus teaches us to live in the world, but not be of it — to bloom with love, no matter the surroundings.

☀️ Life Lesson: Be like the lotus — rooted in truth, rising in devotion, and glowing in divine light.
