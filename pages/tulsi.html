🌿 Tulasi – The Sacred Plant of Krishna

🕉️ Tulasi is Not Just a Plant: Tulasi Devi is worshipped as a goddess and considered the queen of devotion. Every offering to Lord Krishna includes her sacred leaves.

💚 Beloved by Krishna: Krishna says, “I live wherever my devotee offers me Tulasi with love.” Her leaves are more precious than gold to Him.

📖 In Scriptures: She is known as an incarnation of Lakshmi Devi. During Tulasi Vivah, she is married to Krishna, symbolizing divine union.

🌸 Spiritual Meaning: Tulasi represents the heart of a devotee — small, fragrant, humble, and filled with love.
