🦚 Krishna’s Peacock Feather

🌟 Symbol of Divine Grace: Krishna’s crown is never without a peacock feather — it symbolizes purity, playfulness, and divine beauty.

🧘‍♂️ Spiritual Meaning: The feather represents humility, detachment, and the “eye of awareness.” It teaches us to be soft in heart and high in spirit.

📜 From Scriptures: In the Bhagavata Purana, Krishna is described as “wearing a peacock feather and playing his flute to enchant all creation.”

🌈 Colors & Symbolism: The blue reflects compassion, green shows growth and harmony, and the golden shimmer stands for divine radiance — all part of Krishna’s aura.

💫 Life Lesson: Like the feather, be graceful, detached from ego, and full of love. Let Krishna guide you with His divine “eye.”
