Lord Krishna's Leelas

Birth Leela

The miraculous birth of Lord Krishna in Mathura prison...

Lord Krishna was born to Devaki and Vasudeva in the prison of Mathura at midnight on the eighth day of the waning moon in the month of Bhadrapada. His birth was prophesied to end the tyranny of his uncle, King Kamsa. On the night of his birth, the guards fell asleep, and the prison doors unlocked miraculously. Vasudeva carried the newborn Krishna across the Yamuna River to Gokul, where he exchanged him with the daughter of Nanda and Yashoda to protect him from Kamsa's wrath. This divine event marked the beginning of Krishna’s earthly leelas and the fulfillment of cosmic prophecy.
